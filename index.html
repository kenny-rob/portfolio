About Me:

Im currently a student at SAIT participating in their Interactive Design program, focusing on a career in Graphic Design. I’ve always held a passion for using my creativity to better society and to design new experiences that are uniquely human. I’m intrigued by the design industry for its ever-changing adaptability and its talent for creating meaningful connections between corporate brands and average consumers. As a designer I believe collaboration is an essential tool. I enjoy working with people to learn, develop and improve both my technical design and interpersonal skills.
